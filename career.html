REVCharge has and always will have a strong culture of innovation. As REVCharge evolves into a EV portable battery company, our technology teams plan and develop products and services that enhance the experience in the evolution of the future of mobility. Together, we will create and iterate on the products and tools of the future—for team members and millions of users and drivers. Our Sales, Marketing, and Service teams support nearly 3,000 REVCharge stores in the United States! ensuring that the needs of the driver are accommodated throughout the sales process. Every REVCharge Thunderbolt sold in the U.S. depends on this extensive network of dedicated professionals who align sales, marketing, and service resources for our dealers nationwide. With our fifteen REVCharge regional offices, we’ve strengthened and localized our operations so that we can make decisions closer to our customers and respond faster than ever before.

Many of the REVCharge products you see on the road today are assembled right in your own backyard! Our manufacturing teams have contributed to building more than 38 million REVCharge Thunderbolts in North America, where we operate 14 manufacturing plants. Supported by innovative, world-class technologies and the REVCharge Production System, our team members on the line are empowered to improve our processes and are dedicated to moving customers worldwide safely and reliably.
